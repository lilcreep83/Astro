Help

  • Moon Phase: Enter a date and click Calculate to see the moon phase for that day.
  • Stellar Parallax: Enter a parallax angle to find out the distance in parsecs or light-years.
  • Redshift: Compute how much light stretches due to velocity or expansion of the universe.
  • Escape Velocity: Calculate the speed needed to break free from a body's gravity.
  • Gravitational Force: Determine the force between two masses separated by a distance.
  • Angular Size: Find the apparent size of an object given its physical size and distance.
  • E=mc²: Convert mass to energy using Einstein’s famous equation.
  • Universal Gravity: Use Newton’s law to compute gravity anywhere in the universe.
  • Star Magnitude: Estimate a star’s brightness using FITS image data.
  • Planet Positions: View current right ascension, declination, and distance of solar system bodies.
